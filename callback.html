What is Today's Play?


Today's Play is a way to easily discover new music! Just tell us a location and we do the rest: find out who's playing today in your area and create a unique playlist of those artists. Get in touch with your local music scene, or find out the sounds coming from other cities. Every day will be new chance to find your next favorite artist! If you're lucky enough, you may have enough time to catch their show!
